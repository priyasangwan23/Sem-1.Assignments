Veg Biryani

Veg Biryani is a fragrant rice dish cooked with vegetables, basmati rice, and aromatic spices.

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • Spices:
    • Cumin seeds
    • Cloves
    • Cinnamon
    • Bay leaf
  • Onions
  • Ghee or oil

Cooking Steps:

  1. Soak and cook the rice till 70% done.
  2. Prepare vegetable masala with spices.
  3. Layer rice and masala in a pot.
  4. Cook on dum (slow steam).
  5. Serve with raita.

Cooking Terms:

Dum
Slow cooking in a sealed pot with steam.
Tempering
Frying spices in hot oil to release flavors.

Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpeas (chole) served with fried bread (bhature).

Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas (chole)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices:
    • Chole masala
    • Turmeric
    • Coriander powder
  • Bhature dough (maida + yogurt + baking soda)

Cooking Steps:

  1. Soak and boil chickpeas.
  2. Prepare onion-tomato masala with spices.
  3. Add boiled chickpeas and cook well.
  4. Prepare bhature dough and roll into discs.
  5. Deep fry and serve hot with chole.

Cooking Terms:

Knead
Mixing and working the dough until soft and elastic.
Simmer
Cooking food slowly in liquid at a low temperature.

Rasgulla

Rasgulla is a soft and spongy Bengali sweet made from chenna (curdled milk solids) cooked in sugar syrup.

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Lemon juice (to curdle milk)
  • Sugar syrup:
    • Sugar
    • Water
    • Cardamom

Cooking Steps:

  1. Boil milk and curdle it with lemon juice.
  2. Drain and collect chenna, knead till smooth.
  3. Roll into small balls.
  4. Cook in boiling sugar syrup.
  5. Cool and serve chilled.

Cooking Terms:

Chenna
Indian cottage cheese obtained by curdling milk.
Syrup
Sweet liquid made from sugar and water.
