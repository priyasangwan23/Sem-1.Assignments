Mumbai – The City of Dreams

Mumbai is known as the financial capital
of India. It is famous for Bollywood,
Gateway of India, and Marine Drive.
Mumbai, the financial capital of India, is a city that never sleeps.
Known as the "City of Dreams," it is where tradition meets modernity
and where every street has a story to tell. From the iconic Gateway of
India, Marine Drive, and Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus to the lively beaches of
Juhu and the glamour of Bollywood, Mumbai is a city full of contrasts and colors.
It is the land of endless opportunities, resilience, and spirit, where people from
across the country come to chase their dreams. With its bustling markets, colonial architecture,
skyscrapers, and vibrant nightlife, Mumbai is not just a city—it’s an emotion
that lives in the hearts of millions.
